| Competitor | Pricing | Users | Key Weakness | Real User Complaint |

While major gateways offer mature routing (Stripe, Braintree), they require manual UI customization and lack AI-driven adaptability. Conversely, pure SaaS platforms like Lemon Squeezy and RevenueCat offer frontend flexibility but lack deep payment processing power. **tap2 fills the market gap by providing an adaptive, zero-code checkout layer that dynamically optimizes routing and UI in real-time, bridging the gap between developer-level control and business-user simplicity.**

## Trends
- **AI in Fintech**: The AI-in-Fintech market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 23.17% from 2023 to 2030, driven by the increasing demand for personalized and secure payment solutions [AI in Fintech Market](https://www.fortunebusinessinsights.com/ai-in-fintech-market).
- **Global Payment Methods**: The adoption of local payment methods is rising, with 44% of global consumers preferring to pay with local methods [Worldpay Global Payments Report](https://www.worldpay.com/content/dam/worldpay/global/en_us/documents/reports/worldpay-global-payments-report-2022.pdf).
- **Fraud Detection**: Real-time fraud detection is becoming essential, with 65% of merchants reporting an increase in fraud attempts during the pandemic [Pymnts](https://www.pymnts.com/news/payment-methods/2021/merchants-see-increase-in-fraud-attempts-during-pandemic/).

## Key Risks

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **AI Model Drift** | Degradation in routing accuracy and fraud detection over time, leading to increased cart abandonment and fraud losses. | Regularly update AI models with new data and retrain using the latest machine learning techniques. Implement continuous monitoring to detect and address drift promptly. |
| **Integration Failures** | Issues with script integration on merchant sites, leading to broken checkout experiences and lost sales. | Provide comprehensive documentation and support for integration. Offer a sandbox environment for merchants to test the script before going live. |
| **Regulatory Compliance** | Non-compliance with PCI-DSS or other regulations, resulting in fines and loss of customer trust. | Maintain strict adherence to PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance and other relevant regulations. Conduct regular audits and updates to ensure ongoing compliance. |
| **High Transaction Volumes** | Performance degradation during peak times, leading to slow checkout processes and potential loss of sales. | Utilize edge architecture to scale automatically. Offer dedicated bandwidth and custom SLAs for enterprise clients to handle high transaction volumes efficiently. |
